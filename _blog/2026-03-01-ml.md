There are many online courses on machine learning. I would suggest the following:

1- MIT Introduction to Deep Learning | 6.S191 [Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=alfdI7S6wCY&list=PLtBw6njQRU-rwp5__7C0oIVt26ZgjG9NI) 10 videos, each around 50mins. 


2- Stanford CS229: Machine Learning by Andrew Ng | Autumn 2018  [Link](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoROMvodv4rMiGQp3WXShtMGgzqpfVfbU). A similiar one is avaiable on [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/machine-learning-introduction).


3- Hugging Face Course [link](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLo2EIpI_JMQvWfQndUesu0nPBAtZ9gP1o) 80 videos and [material](https://huggingface.co/learn/llm-course/chapter1/1). A more practical aspect of LLM.



Depending on your goal, the following courses are useful:

- Machine Learning Undergraduate Course by Kasper Green Larsen (Aarhus University) [link](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLJWyfGi_JNUvQ7h1o6_iup3Y5X9g2TvxI), 59 Videos. A more mathematical course. 


- Stanford CS224W: Machine Learning with Graphs by Jure Leskovec [Link](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoROMvodv4rPLKxIpqhjhPgdQy7imNkDn) 60 videos.
